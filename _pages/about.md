I'm a CS Ph.D. student at the [University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign](https://illinois.edu) and a member of the [IDEA Lab@UIUC](https://www.idea-isail.com) led by Professor [Hanghang Tong](http://tonghanghang.org). I am broadly interested in data-centric alignment across heterogeneous modalities, especially graph and large language models, aiming to develop methods that enable effective and trustworthy cross-domain modeling.
